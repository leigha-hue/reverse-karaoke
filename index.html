Reverse Karaoke

Can you sing backwards?

How to Play

1

Player 1 Records

Player 1 sings a short clip based on the prompt (10 seconds max).

2

Listen to Reverse

The app reverses Player 1's recording. Listen carefully!

3

Player 2 Mimics

Player 2 tries to mimic the reversed audio (10 seconds max).

4

Reverse Again

The app reverses Player 2's recording back to forward.

5

Get Your Score

The app compares both recordings and gives you a similarity score!

ðŸŽ¯ Tip: The closer Player 2's reversed recording matches Player 1's original, the higher the score!
